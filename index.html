Bramblebanks Township

Welcome to Bramblebanks Township

Bramblebanks Township is 2.5 square miles with a population of 324 at last census. It is located in Glacier county of Montana. Bramblebanks Township is rich in history. Although the town was not founded until 1967, it served for a hub for trading and is an important location for native culture. Most of the residents of Bramblebanks Township are descendants of the Hanaqii tribe, its traditions and way of life valued within the town.

The town welcomes visitors to see a town embracing sustainable agriculture, pre- and post-colonial history, and a healthy local economy and government. We invite you to sample the local establishments including family-owned restaurants, bars, arcades, a shopping plaza, and more.
